Plank: - Instructions: A static exercise where you hold a push-up position with straight arms, engaging the core. - Health benefits: Improves core strength, stability, posture, and overall endurance.

Circle crunch: - Instructions: Similar to a regular crunch, but with a circular motion, engaging the obliques and core muscles from different angles. - Health benefits: Targets the entire abdominal region, enhances core strength, and improves torso flexibility.

Jumping jacks: - Instructions: A cardiovascular exercise performed by jumping with legs spread wide while arms are raised and then returning to the starting position. - Health benefits: Increases heart rate, improves cardiovascular health, and boosts overall endurance.

Bicycle crunch: - Instructions: Involves lying on your back, mimicking a cycling motion while alternating elbow to opposite knee. - Health benefits: Engages the core muscles, improves abdominal strength, and enhances coordination.
