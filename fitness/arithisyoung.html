Plank: - Instructions: A static exercise where you hold a push-up position with straight arms, engaging the core. - Health benefits: Improves core strength, stability, posture, and overall endurance.

Overhead snatch: - Instructions: A weightlifting movement where the barbell is lifted from the ground to overhead in one fluid motion. - Health benefits: Develops explosive power, full-body strength, and coordination while enhancing muscle definition.

Circle crunch: - Instructions: Similar to a regular crunch, but with a circular motion, engaging the obliques and core muscles from different angles. - Health benefits: Targets the entire abdominal region, enhances core strength, and improves torso flexibility.

Back squat: - Instructions: A compound exercise where a barbell is placed on the back while squatting down and standing back up. - Health benefits: Strengthens the lower body, including quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes, while also improving core stability and balance.
