Back squat: - Instructions: A compound exercise where a barbell is placed on the back while squatting down and standing back up. - Health benefits: Strengthens the lower body, including quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes, while also improving core stability and balance.

Clean and jerk: - Instructions: A compound weightlifting exercise involving lifting a barbell from the floor to overhead in two distinct movements. - Health benefits: Enhances full-body strength, power, and coordination while promoting muscle growth and cardiovascular fitness.

Bicycle crunch: - Instructions: Involves lying on your back, mimicking a cycling motion while alternating elbow to opposite knee. - Health benefits: Engages the core muscles, improves abdominal strength, and enhances coordination.

Overhead squat: - Instructions: Similar to a back squat but with the arms extended overhead, challenging stability and mobility. - Health benefits: Improves shoulder flexibility, core strength, and overall squat mechanics, enhancing total body coordination.
