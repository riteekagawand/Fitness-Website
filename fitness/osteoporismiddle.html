Front raises: - Instructions: Involves lifting weights in front of the body with straight arms, targeting the shoulders. - Health benefits: Strengthens the anterior deltoids, improves shoulder stability, and enhances upper body aesthetics.

Walking: - Instructions: A low-impact aerobic exercise involving the repetitive movement of the legs at a moderate pace, typically performed outdoors or on a treadmill. - Health benefits: Improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, aids in weight management, enhances mood, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes.

Air squat bent down: - Instructions: "PLEASE TAKE SOME SUPPORT WHILE DOING THIS EXERCISE". Similar to an air squat, but with a deeper squat position to engage the glutes and hamstrings more. - Health benefits: Targets lower body muscles, improves leg strength, and enhances mobility.

Simple crunch: - Instructions: Involves lying on your back with knees bent, lifting your upper body towards your knees. - Health benefits: Targets the rectus abdominis, improves core strength, and aids in abdominal muscle definition.
